Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Waky (the “App”) handles your personal information.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of the App.

The App requests certain permissions solely to enable essential features:

The App operates entirely on your device. All settings and alarm configurations are stored locally and never transmitted to any server.

5. Data Sharing

6. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of your data being accessed or misused.

8. Your Rights

You retain full control of your data as it is stored only on your device. If you uninstall the App, all associated data will be deleted.
